Kurn enters a small clearing.
The orb of light reveals an eerie seen.
Several scarecrow like figures hang
from the branches, ropes around their
necks, heads tilted, faces hidden in
the darkness of their hoods.

As Kurn watches, crows fly into the
clearing, one for each of the scarecrows
They place glowing gems within the darkened
cowls of the scarecrows.

The scarecrows jerk to life. They reach up
to the ropes they hang from and quickly
scramble up into the darkness of the
overhanging branches.
